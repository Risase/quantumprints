What We Work With

MATERIALS
OF THE WORKSHOP

Industrial-grade filaments for consistent, reliable prints. Choose based on your application's needs.

Most Produced

PLA

POLYLACTIC ACID · FDM · STANDARD
StrengthHigh
FlexibilityRigid
Heat~60°C
DifficultyEasy

Go-to for prototypes, display models, enclosures, and everyday parts. Biodegradable, high detail, multiple colours. Best for indoor use.

Battle-Hardened

PETG

POLYETHYLENE TEREPHTHALATE GLYCOL · FDM
StrengthVery High
FlexibilitySemi-Flex
Heat~80°C
DifficultyModerate

Engineering-grade toughness meets ease of printing. Impact-resistant, slightly flexible. Perfect for mechanical parts, outdoor fixtures, and food-safe applications.

⚔ Incoming to the Workshop

Expanding our arsenal. TPU (flexible), ABS, and specialty filaments are being battle-tested. Gold Patrons get first access at launch.
